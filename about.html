About LINX Building Trust Since 2005

With nearly two decades of experience, we've built a reputation for reliability, quality, and exceptional service in heavy equipment rental across the UAE.

Our Story

Pioneering Heavy Equipment Solutions

Founded in 2005, LINX General Transporting and Contracting began as a small fleet operation serving local construction projects in Dubai. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in heavy equipment rental and transportation services across the United Arab Emirates.

Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: deliver reliable equipment and exceptional service, every time. This commitment has earned us long-term partnerships with some of the region's largest construction firms, oil field operators, and infrastructure developers.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of 150+ vehicles and equipment units, backed by a team of experienced professionals dedicated to supporting your project from start to finish.

The Principles That Guide Us

Reliability

We ensure that all our equipment is meticulously maintained and dependable, providing our clients with consistent performance and peace of mind. Our preventive maintenance programs and regular inspections guarantee equipment availability when you need it most.

Customer Focus

We are dedicated to understanding and meeting the unique needs of each client, delivering personalized service and support to ensure their success. Our team works as an extension of yours, providing solutions tailored to your specific project requirements.
